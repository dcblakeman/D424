College Course Tracker helps students organize their academic schedule in a simple, focused workspace.

Use the app to create terms, add courses, track objective and performance assessments, save course notes, and review important dates in one place. Built-in reminders help you stay on top of term dates, course milestones, and assessment deadlines.

- Add courses with date tracking and status details

- Save and review course notes
- Manage instructor details

- Search terms and courses quickly
- Use reminders to stay aware of important deadlines

Designed for students who want a straightforward way to keep coursework organized without extra clutter.
